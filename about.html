Our story

Dallas's Trusted Carpentry Experts

Our mission is to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and outstanding customer service, transforming homes with quality woodwork that stands the test of time.

Carpenter WE TRUST was founded in 2010 by a team of skilled carpenters who believed in doing the job right the first time. Starting with small repairs, we built a reputation for precision, reliability, and honest pricing. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service carpentry company serving homeowners across Dallas.

Every project, whether a custom built-in or a simple repair, receives the same dedication to quality. We use time-honored techniques combined with modern tools to create beautiful, functional spaces.
