Our story

Craftsmanship Rooted in Dallas

Our mission is to deliver exceptional carpentry that enhances homes and businesses in Dallas, combining old-world skill with modern efficiency.

Carpenter WE TRUST was founded in 2010 by a team of master carpenters who wanted to bring quality woodwork back to the forefront. Starting with small furniture commissions, we quickly earned a reputation for precision and reliability. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service carpentry company serving residential and commercial clients across Dallas.

Every project we take on reflects our commitment to excellence. We invest in continuous training and use only the finest materials. From historic restorations in M Streets to modern builds in Uptown, our work stands the test of time. We believe that good carpentry is an art, and we treat every piece of wood with respect.
