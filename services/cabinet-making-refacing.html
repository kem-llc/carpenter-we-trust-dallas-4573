Cabinet Making & Refacing

Custom kitchen and bathroom cabinets, plus refacing to refresh your existing cabinets.

Schedule this service

Overview

Whether you need brand-new custom cabinets for a kitchen remodel or want to give your existing cabinets a facelift with refacing, we deliver exceptional results. Our cabinets feature dovetail drawers, soft-close hinges, and durable finishes. Refacing includes new doors, drawer fronts, and hardware, plus a fresh veneer on the cabinet boxes.

What's included

  • Custom sizes to fit any kitchen layout
  • High-quality hardware and finishes
  • Refacing saves up to 50% vs. new cabinets
  • Minimal disruption during installation
