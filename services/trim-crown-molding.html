Trim & Crown Molding

Add elegance to any room with professional trim and crown molding installation.

Schedule this service

Overview

Crown molding, baseboards, chair rails, and wainscoting can transform a plain room into a showpiece. Our carpenters have extensive experience with intricate trim work, including coping inside corners for seamless joints. We work with MDF, solid wood, and polyurethane materials to match your budget and style.

What's included

  • Increased home value and curb appeal
  • Flawless joints and smooth finishes
  • Custom profiles and designs available
  • Painting and staining services included

How it works

1

Measurement & Material Selection

We measure your rooms and help you choose the right trim style and material.

2

Installation

Our team installs the trim with precision, coping corners for a perfect fit.

3

Finishing

We caulk, sand, and paint or stain the trim to match your décor.
