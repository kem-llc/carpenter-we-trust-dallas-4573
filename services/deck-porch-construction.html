Deck & Porch Construction

Build beautiful, durable decks and porches for outdoor living.

Schedule this service

Overview

Extend your living space outdoors with a custom deck or porch. We design and build structures using pressure-treated lumber, cedar, or composite materials. Our decks feature integrated seating, planters, and lighting options. We handle all permits and inspections to ensure safety and code compliance.

What's included

  • Custom design to match your home's architecture
  • Low-maintenance composite options available
  • Built to last with proper drainage and fasteners
  • Includes railings, stairs, and lighting

How it works

1

Site Evaluation & Design

We assess your yard, discuss your needs, and create a design that fits your space and budget.

2

Permitting & Material Order

We obtain necessary permits and order materials. We'll keep you informed every step.

3

Construction

Our crew builds the deck with attention to detail, from footings to final stain.
